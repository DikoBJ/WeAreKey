ABOUT
US

Hotel Elio emerged from the visionary dream of Elias "Elio" Rodriguez, a passionate hotelier with a deep appreciation for luxury and hospitality. Inspired by his travels and a desire to create a unique haven, Elio meticulously planned and designed the hotel to blend classical elegance with contemporary comfort.

FACILITIES

Nestled amidst historic streets and vibrant culture, our boutique hotel boasts a unique blend of modern luxury and timeless elegance. From beautifully appointed rooms and suites to personalized amenities and thoughtful touches, Hotel Elio Sma Amenity is the perfect choice for discerning travelers seeking comfort, convenience, and sophistication.
